QUOTE GENERATION

The fuel quote is generated using certain criteria to our users.

This prediction is done based on Location of the client, Competitors rate, The Fuel Quote History of the client, The number of gallons requested, The Profit Margin of the company and the Seasonal Rate Fluctuation.

DISPLAY FUEL QUOTE HISTORY

The fuel quote history is displayed to our users.

The fuel history of the user is displayed when requested which consists of Date, Number of gallons requested, Suggested Price, Total Due and Delivery address.
